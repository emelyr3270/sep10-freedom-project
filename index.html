Animation

Anitmation is a a way of expressing art through creating the illusion of movement by rapidly displaying a series of steady images. Theres many different types of animation such as 3D, 2D, Motion graphics, claymation, Stop motion and much more. Animation is highly driven by technology, but many people dont know what type of technology is used. Using tech helps animators create and make these drawings come to life. Now what are the tech being used to create animation?

Animators Software

What software do people in animation use?

FlipaClip

FlipaClip is beginner friendly animation software used for frame-by-frame animation. its Easy to adapt to, It looks a bit time consuming but if you put in the effort, it'll get paided off.

Alight Motion

Alight Motion is used for motion graphics. Its used for Mobile and devices and Mac computers. From keyframe animation to video compositing to visual effects you can create anything. From the looks of it, it seems confusing and hard to use, but theres alot of effects and tutorials to help.

Procreate

Procreate is used for 2D Animation. Its purpose is used for both animation and digital drawings. It has many tool options, but its not available for everyone and needs a subscrition to be paid.

Animation Hardware

Theres many types of different animation hardware. But what types of devices do people in animation use?

People in animation use a wide variety of devices For modern digital & 3D animation, animators use: Graphic tablets, styluses, computers, desktops, Monitors, software, Based on the website CUDO COMPUTE The Optimal Hardware Specifications for Animation are: Processor (CPU)- determines how quickly your computer can perform tasks. For animation, a high-speed multi-core processor is best. Graphics Card (GPU)- a graphics card or GPU, renders images, animations, and videos. A powerful GPU is essential for smooth, efficient animation creation RAM- Random Access Memory (RAM) is crucial for animation processes, especially when working with high-resolution assets or complex scenes. Storage- fast storage solution ensures swift loading and saves time. For Traditional and stop-motion animation, animators use: Paper and pencils: Traditional 2D animation still relies on basic tools like paper, pencils, and sometimes easy-erase colored pencils for sketching. Animation software: Programs like Harmony or Pencil2D can be used for digital versions of traditional animation, sometimes referred to as "digital paper" or "digital animation". Stop-motion equipment: For stop-motion, specific setups are needed, including cameras and software, to capture and assemble the individual frames.

Cameras and Scanners

To digitize physical artwork, animators use cameras, scanners, or a rostrum camera to capture images frame by frame.

Light box/table

A portable or professional light table is essential for seeing through multiple layers of paper and cels.
