Animation

Anitmation is a a way of expressing art through creating the illusion of movement by rapidly displaying a series of steady images. Theres many different types of animation such as 3D, 2D, Motion graphics, claymation, Stop motion and much more. Animation is highly driven by technology, but many people dont know what type of technology is used. Using tech helps animators create and make these drawings come to life.
